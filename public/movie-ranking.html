The Best Movies According to Connor

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Blade Runner

I love the story and environment.

Ex Machina

Excellent movie about AI.

Terminator 2

One of the best sci-fi action movies ever made. Excellent special effects that still hold up today.

Alien

The best sci-fi horror movie of all time. Always fresh and enjoyable.

Akira

Japan's Blade Runner. Very influential to all anime that came after it.
